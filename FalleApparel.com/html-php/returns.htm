Return Policy


What is your return policy?

We accept returned merchandise in its original condition, along with the original invoice for a refund of the purchase price.

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 14 calendar days from the delivery date to send an item back.

When do I get reimbursed for my order?

Your payment will be refunded within 7 days of receiving your return item.

Are delivery charges refundable?

No, original delivery charges are non-refundable.

Can I exchange an item?

If you want to exchange an item, you will need to return the original item and place a new order.
